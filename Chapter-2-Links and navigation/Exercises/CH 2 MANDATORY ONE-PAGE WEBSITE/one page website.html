PLOT OVERVIEW

'Brave Citizen', a South Korean film released in 2023, is a story follows the main character, So Si-min, a former female boxing champion who works as a contract high school teacher at Mooyoung High. In the movie, Mooyoung High is portrayed as an 'exemplary anti-bullying school' in the eyes of the public, when in reality it's the exact opposite. Early into the movie and So Si-min's job, she finds out the overwhelming truth about the bullying that takes place in the high school, which had caused a lot of previous contract teachers to end their lives. This leads us to our main antagonist, Han Su-kang. Wealthy, protected, and severely feared by the students and the faculty, Su-kang and his crew face no consequences when they harass and bully students for their entertainment. Not wanting to lose her job but unable to let injustice slide, the former boxing champion disguises herself as a man with a cat mask and teaches Su-kang a lesson everyone secretly yearned for, becoming a hero in Mooyoung High.

KEY INFORMATION

Brave Citizen is a South Korean action drama movie released On October 25, 2023. It was directed by Park Jin-Pyo.

  • Total run time: 113 minutes.
  • Original language: Korean.
  • Based on the webcomic "Yongamhan Shimin" by Kim Jung-Hyun, published in 2014.​
  • Filming commenced on December 30, 2021, and concluded on April 5, 2022.
  • "Brave Citizen" is available for rent or purchase on Prime Video.
