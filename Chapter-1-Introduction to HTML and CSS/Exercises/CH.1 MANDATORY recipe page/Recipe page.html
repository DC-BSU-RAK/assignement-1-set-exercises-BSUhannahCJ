Creamy Sun Dried Tomato Chicken

Pan fried chicken cooked in a creamy sun dried tomato pesto. Goes perfectly with a side of rice and saut√©ed asparagus, roasted potatoes, or plain cooked pasta. This recipe can feed up to 5 people. Easy to cook and a family favourite at iftar dinners. (spices can be adjusted to your liking)

Ingredients

For the chicken

  • Fresh chicken fillets (400g)
  • Paprika (1 tsp)
  • Dried oregano (1 tsp)
  • Garlic granules (1 tsp)
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Olive oil (1 tbsp)

For the sauce

  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • chilli flakes (1/2 tsp)
  • Dried oregano (1 tsp)
  • cooking cream (250 ml)
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Sun dried tomato pesto (190 g)
  • Handful of baby spinach (optional)
  • Olive oil (1 tbsp)
  • Water (30 ml)

Steps

For the chicken

  • Cut up your chicken to desired size
  • Add all of the spices and olive oil to a bowl and mixed
  • Rub the mixed spices and olive oil all over the chicken
  • Marinate for an hour (optional)
  • Pan fry, oven bake, or air fry until cooked

For the sauce

  • Heat olive oil in pan
  • Add in minced garlic and chilli flakes to the pan and fry for a minute
  • Then add the sun dried tomato pesto and cooking cream, water, spinach, spices, and herbs mentioned above into the same pan and stir well
  • add in the cooked chicken and let it simmer until sauce has thickened
  • serve with your side of choice and enjoy
